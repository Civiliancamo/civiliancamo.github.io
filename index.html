About CivCam

"Civilian Camo is a two piece heavy rock band consisting of Ian Wiedrick (Water Torture, Snapcase) and Christy Peixoto (XIY) based in Brooklyn, NY. Coming off a full length release last year, they recently completed a new EP recorded by Mario Quintero (Spotlights) at The Creamery Studios. A full on attack of thunderous riffs and deafening drums, Civilian Camo explores the depths of heavy music while bringing memorable anthems to the table. Check out the EP streaming now on Spotify and Bandcamp!"
