About CivCam

***Formed a few years back and finally come to fruition during the lockdown of 2020, the Brooklyn based 2 piece noise rock duo Civilian Camo is the conceptual lovechild of guitarist vocalist Ian Wiedrick and drummer Christy Peixoto. CivCam strives to blatantly pummel your ears as they craft quick dizzying assaults of feedback drenched hard rock tailored for contemporary attention spans. Sometimes melodic sometimes downright chaotic, the dystopian narrative spun around lyrical self disgust helps to create an atmosphere echoing late eighties NY no-wave and early 90's alternative grunge. This is a not so subtle call to keep it heavy, angry, and loud. All while basking in the state of the modern world as we collectively spiral out into oblivion***
